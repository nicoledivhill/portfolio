All the Details

My interests in web design started from a pretty early age when I joined the “Geek Squad” and fixed computers for my teachers in the 5th grade. Ever since then I’ve been able to explore technology through childhood vlogs and social media. I have yet to experience the real world of design but am really excited for my career and how my path lays out. Outside of designing I find myself attempting to get as far away from my computer as possible and heal my eyes. I find myself working out, painting, cooking, or just getting some fresh air outside.

Softball has been majority of my life and it's where I've learned almost all life lessons. I've delt with adversity, team struggles, and indiduval doubt and failure. Throughout the 15 years, I've learned to be resiliant and confident when it comes to facing challenges, quiet and observant in moments of growth and most importantly, loving and kind to those around me because you never know what type of impact you have on someone.
