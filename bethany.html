    Client

    Bethany's Boba Tea & Cafe in Arlington, TX is a cozy, family-owned spot known for its wide selection of Asian-inspired dishes and signature boba drinks. The relaxed atmosphere, paired with friendly service, makes it an ideal hangout for students and locals.

    They struggled with trying to create a website that was clear and concise. There wasn't much of a theme to the site so our goal was to display it in a way that was much easier to the user.

    Reflection

    What was successful?

    The teamwork my partner and I had in order to complete the project and create cohesiveness throughout the site was very successful. Our integrated internet marketing techiques would also be very successful if they were fully implemented.

    What would I change?

    Because there are elements that aren't completely developed, I wish I had more time to fully develop those details. I also would have utilized GitHub in order to help with the collaboration aspect of this project.
